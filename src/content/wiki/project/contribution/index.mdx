This page will collect the tools, datasets, protocols, design decisions, and lessons that can help future iGEM teams or researchers build on our work.

## Expected Contributions

- A searchable PETase candidate discovery workflow.
- Documentation for interpreting PETadex results.
- Assay and validation notes from wet lab work.
- Hardware design notes for controlled enzyme testing.
